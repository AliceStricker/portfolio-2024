What particularly fascinates me in this field is the power of sound in audiovisual. In my opinion, sound is even more crucial than image. It has the unique ability to physically affect the viewer, hurt or relax them through certain frequencies, and also has strong emotional power. A soundtrack without images opens the viewer's imagination and allows them to escape. It is this experience that I want to live and master, in order to be able to create my own impactful projects in the future.

-Alice Stricker
